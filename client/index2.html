About Us

The Journal of Politics, published annually by the Registrar of Dibrugarh University, has been a cornerstone of academic discourse in the field of Political Science since its inception in 1987. Under the expert editorship of Dr. Obja Borah Hazarika, the journal has maintained a tradition of excellence, providing a platform for scholars and practitioners to engage with cutting-edge research and developments within the discipline. Available in both print and online formats, the journal ensures wide accessibility and dissemination of knowledge.

We are committed to engaging our readers by providing insightful, thought-provoking content that addresses both timeless political theories and pressing contemporary issues. Our diverse range of articles appeals to scholars, practitioners, and anyone interested in the complex world of politics.
